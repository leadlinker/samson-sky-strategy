The Problems

Now that we know what is there, here is what those findings reveal about what is costing you reservations right now.

Problem 1

Your Ads Are Burning Budget Without a Conversion Funnel

You are running 8 Google Ads across video, search, and display. That is real investment. But every ad points to the generic homepage, not a dedicated landing page designed to convert paid traffic into captured leads or reservation deposits.

Here is what the buyer journey looks like today:

1

Google Ad Click

A potential buyer sees "Switchblade Flying Sports Car" in a Google ad. They are intrigued. They click.

2

Homepage Landing

They arrive at samsonsky.com. They see the hero section, maybe watch a video. The product looks incredible.

3

No Clear Next Step

There is no lead magnet, no "download the buyer's guide," no email gate offering specs and pricing in exchange for contact info.

4

Tab Closed

They leave. No email captured. No way to retarget them. No follow-up possible. The ad spend that got them there is gone.

You are paying to drive traffic to a page that has no system for capturing the interest you are generating.
Problem 2

No Nurture Path for a $170K Decision

The Switchblade costs $170,000. Even the reservation deposit is $2,000. This is not an impulse purchase. Buyers research for months, sometimes years.

No Content Delivered After Signup

We tested the newsletter signup. After subscribing, nothing was received. No welcome email, no specs PDF, no timeline update, no founder story. The moment of highest interest passes without reinforcement.

No Drip Sequence Visible

Based on what we can see from the visitor side, there is no automated email sequence educating prospects about the Switchblade, the Builder Assist Program, or the timeline to delivery. A $170K purchase needs dozens of touchpoints over months.

No Segmentation Between Curious and Serious

Someone who found you through a Google Ad and someone who already has a $2,000 deposit down are experiencing the same website with the same content.

The interest is there. 2,700 reservations prove it. What is missing is the system that moves the next 2,700 from "this is incredible" to "I am placing my deposit."
Problem 3

$600M in Pipeline With No Retargeting Infrastructure

You have nearly 2,700 reservations representing over $600M in future revenue. You are running paid ads. You have press coverage from AOPA, Flying Magazine, Robb Report, New Atlas, and others.

What Is Missing

No Facebook Pixel, no LinkedIn Insight Tag, and no retargeting infrastructure. Every visitor who leaves without converting is gone forever. You cannot build lookalike audiences, retarget ad clickers, or measure which traffic sources produce reservation-ready visitors. The data to optimize your entire acquisition strategy is not being collected.

Every day the pixels are not installed is a day of lost audience intelligence.
